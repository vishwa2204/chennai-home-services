About Chennai Home Service

Provide Best and Budget Fit Services in Chennai

Chennai Home Service, established in 2008, has been a trusted provider of home maintenance solutions for over 15 years. Initially focusing on electrical and plumbing services for over 100 buildings, they expanded their offerings in 2013 to include a wider range of services such as AC, refrigerator, washing machine, RO, home need cleaning, and CCTV installation, as well as interior design.
With over a decade of experience, they specialize in servicing ACs, refrigerators, and washing machines, and also offer sales of both new and used appliances. For the past five years, they have been actively involved in sales, providing reconditioned spare parts for ACs, refrigerators, and washing machines. As a cost-effective option for repairs, they also purchase old appliances for parts. Furthermore, they buy and sell old and scrap ACs, refrigerators, and washing machines.
